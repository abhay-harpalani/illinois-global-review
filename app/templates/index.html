About Us

Founded in 2023, the Illinois Global Review (IGR) began as a passion project among international relations students who wanted a space to share their voices, opinions, and research on global events. What started as a small initiative has since grown into a campus-wide publication dedicated to making international affairs accessible and engaging for all students. At its core, IGR empowers young thinkers to express their perspectives confidently and contribute meaningfully to conversations shaping our world.
